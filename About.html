About_Us

Save Life India is an online database of Voluntary blood donor's network across India who like to help needy people by donating blood.

Health and Wealth is Best Way

Save Life India is a service project under the 'Volunteer for a Better India' (VBI) initiative of the 'Art of Living' organisation. It is led by a volunteer team from Goa. VBI inspires citizens of India to give 'One Hour to the Nation' and make community service a way of life.
